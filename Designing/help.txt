Magnification
____________

Accessibility>Magnification>Magnification shortcut


If you haven't already first turn on the magnification shortcut which lies in your device's settings. This allows you to zoom in on any screen by clicking on the accessibility icon on the bottom right. You can move around the screen by dragging the 2 fingers or adjust zoom by pinching the 2 fingers. Use the icon to stop the magnification.
